Cubic Engineering Consultancy (CEC) is an engineering and architectural consultancy based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. CEC supports clients across the full project lifecycle, including design, feasibility studies, planning, project management and construction supervision.

CEC focuses on solving complex engineering challenges, maintaining high quality standards, and building long-term partnerships with its clients. The company emphasizes honesty, responsibility, transparency and customer care as core values.

In internal LLM and RAG experiments, CEC uses AI assistants to help engineers navigate documentation, summarize technical notes and speed up access to project information. However, final design decisions are always made by qualified human engineers.
